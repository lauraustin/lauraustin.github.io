I am a recent graduate of Columbia University, where I studied English and linguistics. During this time, I gained experience writing in all genres, including playwriting, interactive fiction, literary criticism, and more. My theatrical work has been published by University of Pennsylvania's La Vida magazine, and my critical essay "#RichKidsofTehran: Zoom Theatre, Remediation, and Interactivity" recieved Columbia University's Bunner Prize. I have also spearheaded several marketing campaigns and creative projects, having worked for Barnard's Pandemic Panels as well as led Columbia Int Fic, an interactive fiction collective. In my spare time I can be found scrolling TikTok, learning languages, or rollerskating.
