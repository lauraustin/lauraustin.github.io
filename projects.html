Head in the Clouds

An imaginative take on the mundane, Head in the Clouds illustrates how imagination is not always the wonderland associated with childhood, but can also be a source of anxiety as well as escapism. This piece is accompanied by original music and images.

Trash 2020

"I wonder if people are more like paper or plastic. When they are thrown away, do they give in to the earth or struggle against it indefinitely?" This piece, which takes place within Google Calendar, examines waste from a new perspective.

The Salt of Labor

This collective project tells the day in the life of two characters from Monique Truong's Book of Salt. It utilizes a unique, dual-dialogue structure and is accompanied by original images.

Columbia Int Fic: What If

CIF's What If is a series of 10 interactive narratives from student-writers, programmers, and artists dealing with Covid pandemic. In addition to leading the general team and contributing writing for two of the stories, I also led the tech team.
